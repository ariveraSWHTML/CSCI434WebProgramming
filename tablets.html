Tablets and Laptops

Unlike Desktop computers, mobile devices such as phones, laptops, and tablets provide a huge convenience for the consumer. Conveniences like being able to walk into a coffee shop or on the road on a metro, the ability to be able to check on email, work, and social accounts freely and easily makes these devices successful. A tablet or Laptop offers competition to the desktop when it comes to using applications for work or entertainment. While laptops or tablets are not as powerful as the desktop, the trade-off of convenience and ease of use is typically the trade-off.



Are they better than Desktops?

Laptops and tablets have been in competition with Desktop computers for many years. While devices like laptops and tablets are significantly smaller and cannot be equipped with hardware compared to computers, their performance side by side can still be compareable. While laptops might not be able to handle 8K video, they can still perform pretty well where consumers might not care about the increased pixels or frames per second.
