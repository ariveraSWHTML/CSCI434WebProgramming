The Why and How-to build a Gaming Computer

Why build your own gaming computer? Well, besides potential cost savings, building a computer can be a great experiece and rewarding yet stressful at the same time. Fortunately, there are many resources out there to assist you in building a gaming pc e.g. a 10 minute How-To tutorial on Youtube (link below). So what will you need to build a computer besides a positive attitude and desire? Let's talk about the main components.

There are several important components that you will need to research specifically when it comes to building a gaming desktop. Sure, you can save time and buy what a pre-built gaming systems but these may not have some of the upgraded components you may want. One of these components is a video card, due to a recent chip shortage, these video cards are on high demand and some are overpriced and sold individually. Of these video cards, you can choose a top of the line video card either the Nvidia RTX 3080 or the AMD Radeon RX 6800 XT. Video cards are important when it comes to the level and detail for not just video games but video and web design as well. Although video cards are important when it comes to graphics processing, you will need a processing unit that can manage the load.

The Central Processing Unit, or CPU, the brain of a computer, that executes logic to make them function. Everything from keyboard, mouse, and gamepad inputs all get processed by the CPU. Therefore you can imagine, that with a weak CPU then a owner cannot expect to accomplish many tasks simultaneuously. This is why it is one of the most critical parts that needs to be powerful enough to manage gaming and video/photo editing over 1080p.
