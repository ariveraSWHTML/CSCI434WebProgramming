Monitors

Laptops and tablets are convenient in that you don't need a display separate from the device. Desktops however require external video equipments, i.e. Monitors, to output information such as images or text. There are many different types of displays, big or small, slow or fast, high dynamic range versus standard definition, you name it! The type of monitor you may require depends a lot on what your requirement or pleasure is.



Monitors then and now!

Monitors have been around since the first CRT, then later upgraded to the LCD, then LED etc. These monitors have been upgraded in line with increased technology and materials. Some monitors also offer a competitive edge specifically for gaming where a user running a game at 240hz may gain the upper hand with someone playing on a 60hz screen.
