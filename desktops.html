Desktop Computers

Desktop computers are the go to when it comes to work productivity requiring maximum performance. Unlike laptops or tablets, Desktop computers require additional peripherals such as a keyboard, mouse, and monitors. While a desktop has it's advantages in performance and cost, there are also disadvantages such as ease of mobility, weight, and peripheral restrictions.



Different forms: All In Ones, Small, Medium, or Large!

Desktop computers come in different forms as well such as the all-in-one. This type of computer looks like a monitor but is in fact a desktop with components such as the Central Processing Unit CPU, Motherboard, Video Cards, and Random Access Memory (RAM) all integrated inside. There are also what are called form factors where computers can range anywhere from 5-30 lbs depending on the equipment you want in them.
